Tesla name and logo

The name Tesla Corporation bears the name of one
of the greatest scientists of the beginning of the last century,
Nikola Tesla. He is often referred to as the second Leonardo da Vinci,
"Lord of Lightning", "Mysterious Genius"
and "Tamer of Light" It was thanks to Tesla's bold ideas
that the large-scale electrification of our planet began.
Therefore, it is not surprising that the
founders of an electric car company decided to name
their company after the man who did so much to
“open” electricity to the world.
